Broccoli Cauliflower Casserole

Description

This broccoli cauliflower casserole is a delightful medley of creamy, cheesy goodness with a touch of earthy freshness. The tender broccoli and cauliflower bring a subtle, nutty flavor that pairs beautifully with the rich, velvety cheese sauce. Hints of garlic, onion, or other seasonings enhance the vegetables' natural sweetness, while a golden, crispy topping—whether breadcrumbs, Parmesan, or both—adds a satisfying crunch. Each bite is a perfect balance of savory, creamy, and comforting flavors, making it a dish that is hearty yet light, and irresistibly delicious.

  • 2 cups broccoli
  • 2 cups cauliflower
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese, divided
  • ½ cup mayonnaise
  • ½ cup chopped onion
  • ½ cup butter, melted
  • 1 (15 ounce) package seasoned croutons, crushed

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Place broccoli and cauliflower in separate saucepans, cover with water, and bring to a boil; cook until tender, 3 to 4 minutes. Drain and arrange in an 11x7-inch baking dish.
  3. Beat eggs in a bowl until creamy. Stir in 1/2 cup Cheddar cheese, mayonnaise, and onion; pour over vegetables. Pour melted butter on top and sprinkle with remaining 1/2 cup Cheddar cheese. Scatter crushed croutons evenly on top.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until browned and bubbling, about 40 minutes.
