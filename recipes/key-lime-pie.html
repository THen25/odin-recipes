Key Lime Pie

Description

This Key Lime Pie is bright, creamy, and irresistibly refreshing. Each bite delivers a perfect balance of tangy citrus and smooth sweetness, made with freshly squeezed key lime juice for that signature zesty kick. The filling is luxuriously silky, nestled inside a buttery, golden graham cracker crust that adds just the right amount of crunch. Finished with a cloud of lightly sweetened whipped cream, this pie is cool, vibrant, and bursting with tropical flavor. It’s the kind of dessert that feels like sunshine on a plate—simple, classic, and absolutely unforgettable.

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups graham cracker crumbs
  • ⅓ cup granulated sugar
  • 6 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 (14 oz) can sweetened condensed milk
  • ½ cup fresh key lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon lime zest
  • 3 large egg yolks
  • 1 cup heavy whipping cream
  • 2 tablespoons powdered sugar

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a medium bowl, mix the graham cracker crumbs, granulated sugar, and melted butter until evenly combined.
  3. Press the mixture firmly into the bottom and up the sides of a 9-inch pie dish to form the crust.
  4. Bake the crust for 8–10 minutes, then remove from the oven and let it cool slightly.
  5. In a separate bowl, whisk together the sweetened condensed milk, key lime juice, lime zest, and egg yolks until smooth and creamy.
  6. Pour the filling into the warm crust and spread evenly.
  7. Bake for 12–15 minutes, or until the center is set but still slightly jiggly.
  8. Remove from the oven and let the pie cool at room temperature for 30 minutes.
  9. Refrigerate for at least 3 hours, or until fully chilled and set.
  10. Before serving, whip the heavy cream and powdered sugar until soft peaks form, then spread or pipe over the pie.
  11. Slice, serve chilled, and enjoy!
