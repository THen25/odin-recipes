Baked Ziti

Description

This baked ziti recipe is a symphony of comforting flavors and textures, blending tender ziti pasta with a rich and savory tomato sauce infused with aromatic garlic, onions, and Italian herbs. Layers of gooey melted mozzarella, creamy ricotta, and sharp Parmesan create a decadent, cheesy blanket that browns beautifully in the oven. The dish is hearty and satisfying, with a golden crust on top and a bubbling, saucy interior. Perfectly balanced between tangy tomato, savory cheese, and pasta, its a crowd-pleasing meal that feels like a warm embrace. Whether enjoyed fresh out of the oven or as leftovers, its a recipe that delivers joy in every bite.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound dry ziti pasta
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 2 (26 ounce) jars spaghetti sauce
  • 6 ounces provolone cheese, sliced
  • 1 Â½ cups sour cream
  • 6 ounces mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add ziti pasta, and cook until al dente, about 8 minutes; drain.
  3. Meanwhile, brown ground beef and onion in a large skillet over medium heat; stir in spaghetti sauce and simmer for 15 minutes. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Butter a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  4. Spread 1/2 of the ziti in the bottom of the prepared dish.
  5. Top with Provolone cheese.
  6. Top with sour cream.
  7. Add 1/2 of the meat sauce, remaining ziti, mozzarella cheese, and remaining meat sauce. Top with grated Parmesan cheese.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven until heated through and cheeses have melted, about 30 minutes.
